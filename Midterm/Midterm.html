Competitive video games

My love for video games and my inner competitive personality birthed my interest and love for competitive video games!

Growing up with the internet, competitive video games has been a hobby of mine ever since middle school. Through it, it has introduced me to people from all over the world who I would have never met otherwise and has helped me make friends who i've now known for over 10 years. My competitive gaming history consists mainly of first person shooter (FPS) games.

Competitive gaming history

  • Unreal Tournament series
  • Quake series
  • Counter strike series
  • GunZ: The Duel
  • Team Fortress 2
  • Overwatch
  • Fortnite
  • Super Smash Bros. Melee

Competitive video games has developed my personality and mentality, and has played a big role into becoming who I am today.

Computers

Being given a computer at an early age, growing up i've almost always been surrounded and interested in technology, most specifically computers.

With exposure to computers from my cousins, curiousity grew which in turn turned into interest. I began learning the ins and outs of computers sometime in middle school, which led to disassembly and assembly of computers, and from there thirst for knowledge in computers grew. I eventually was trouble shooting computers and repairing them for my friend's and family and as soon as I knew, I quickly became their technology technician.

As of recent, I've been learning and have been getting into computer programming/coding. I've taken several computer science classes through my college with great professors and now work as a computer science tutor at my college.
